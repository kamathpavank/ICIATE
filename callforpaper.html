Submitting an Abstract

  1. Create your account on Easychair by clicking on this link
  2. Submit an abstract to here till 1st Nov’2016.

Submitting an Paper

Submit your paper till 20th November.
Earlier submission helps us to manage the review process on time
This is of particular importance if you will need a visa to attend the conference.

Documents

  1. Documents should be in zipped file with name“ICIATE_TrackNo_paperID”
  2. Camera ready paper (.docx, .doc)
  3. Scanned copy of the copyright form of IJERT and ICIATE-2017
  4. Scanned copy of registration form
  5. Scanned Copy of Payment Receipt (Generated receipt after registration)
  6. Scanned copy of Student ID/ Faculty ID

Documents To Be Carried During the Day of Conference

Authors are informed to bring the following original documents on the day of conference.

  1. Copyright form of IJERT and ICIATE-2017
  2. Registration form
  3. Payment Receipt (Generated receipt after registration)
  4. Xerox of Student ID/ Faculty ID
